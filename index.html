How It Works

From Brief to Render

01
Book a Consultation

Reach out via the contact form. I'll get back to you to schedule a 30-minute consultation slot.

02
Brief & Plans

Share your floor plans, mood board and material palette. We'll align on scope and set a clear timeline together.

03
Discovery Questions

I'll ask you a focused set of questions about your project — style preferences, lighting feel, colour tones, and references — so the render captures exactly the look and atmosphere you have in mind.

04
Rendering (~3 Days)

Work begins. Most projects are turned around in approximately 3 days — more complex scenes may take a little longer. You'll be kept in the loop throughout.

05
First Delivery

Your renders are delivered in high resolution. Take your time to review them carefully against your original vision.

06
2 Revision Rounds

Not quite right? Every project includes two rounds of revisions — adjustments to lighting, colours, furniture, or composition until it feels perfect.

07
Finalised Photos

Once you're happy, the finalised high-res renders are yours — clean, polished, and exactly as envisioned.
